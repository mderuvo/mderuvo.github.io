AI guided early detection of Alzheimer's and Parkinson's

At CogniScent we couple scientific research with machine learning expertise to provide real time risk assessment

Find Out More

Early diagnosis of neurodegenerative diseases
is urgently needed

The current tests for AD and PD detection are costly, time consuming and focused on late stages of the disease. At CogniScent we use AI to design non invasive and real time tests that focuse on early symptoms and olfactory performance, speeding up diagnosis and cutting medical costs.

How it works

Insert your data on our app


Our test presents questions on your olfactory abilities and early symptoms. Thanks to the easy-to-use and inclusive interface of our app, patients can upload data by themselves or with the help of a nurse. We take care of data format and ensure data privacy

Our algorithm performs predictions

Using accurate machine learning algorithms, we identify patterns that are central to the classification of patients at risk

Real Time Diagnosis


We compute risk assessments (low, medium, high) for Alzheimer's and Parkinson's and based on the results, physicians give recommendations on further steps
